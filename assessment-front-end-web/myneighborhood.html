Burlington, Vermont

Welcome to my beautiful neighborhood in Burlington, VT. Burlington is the most populous town in Vermont with a population of around 40,000. Burlington has a unique culture inspired by art, music, and food. Many people are drawn to Burlington each year to explore the culture with events such as the Jazz Festival, Restaurant Week, and of course the Beer Festival. Burlington also attracts many famous musicians and entertainers, but still maintains an emphasis on local arts. You can't mention Burlington without talking about beer. Vermont is home to over 40 microbreweries that produce some of the best beer in the world. Some of the most notable breweries: Zero Gravity, Foam Brewers, and Burlington Beer Company are all located in Burlington.
